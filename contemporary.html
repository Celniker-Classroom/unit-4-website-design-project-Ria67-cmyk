Information about Contemporary

Contemporary is a style that incorporates technique from ballet and jazz. It focuses on a connection between the mind and body, and emphasizes fluidity. Some things worked on in contemporary are

  • Fluidity(being able to melt into movements)
  • Improvisation(being able to create choreography on the spot)
  • Quick choreography pickup(this is one of the styles where learning combos is most common, so choreography pick up is heavily worked on, similiar to Jazz)
Other things incorporated and learned through this style are floorwork, self expression, flexibility, strength, technique and storytelling through dance. Dancers often perform this style barefoot, and there is less an emphasis on technique but more an emphasis on fluidity and making the choreography big. If you want to do a more expressive style, and want to learn to nail choreography in 5 minutes, contemporary is the style for you.
