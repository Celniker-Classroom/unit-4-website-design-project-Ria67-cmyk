Jazz is a style that incorporates technique from ballet. It focuses on dynamic and energetic movements and is more fast paced than most styles. Some things worked on in Jazz are

  • Flexibility and strength excercises(used for dynamic leaps and turns)
  • Power in dance(being able to keep energy throughout a whole combo)
  • Quick choreography pickup(like contemporary, learning combos is common in Jazz)

This style also focuses on self expression and technique, to make sure that your dance has an equal amount of everything. If you want a more dynamic and energetic style, Jazz is for you.
